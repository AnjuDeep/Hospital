Neurology

At the neurology department we offer most accurate diagnosis and medical management of common to complex neurological conditions. We take a multidisciplinary approach in diagnosis and treatment of neurological conditions at the department, providing individual care to patients with diseases such as stroke, epilepsy, movement disorders, sleep disorders, and tumors of the brain, spine and nerves.
