Urology

The specialists in EKA has the expertise to diagnose and treat complex urological disorders in both children as well as adults. The Urology department at EKA provides advanced diagnosis, treatment and surgical procedures for all kidney‐related problems. We provide urological care for infertility, erectile dysfunction, urological cancers, congenital and stone diseases.Our doctors leverage their combined experience and knowledge to provide specialised care to our patients suffering from cancer and other urological problems. Apart from General Urology, the department has dedicated units for Endourology, Renal Transplantation, Paediatric Urology and Laparoscopic Urology and Reconstructive Urology.
