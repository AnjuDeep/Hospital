Obstetrics & Gynec Oncology

We provide women with comprehensive and compassionate diagnosis and treatment options at every stage of their lives. The talented team of obstetrics and gynaecologists at the department provide comprehensive treatment, and are capable of supporting women throughout their life starting from pre‐pregnancy period to menopause and beyond. We help pregnant women deliver healthy babies by detecting birth defects and genetic conditions early through targeted ultrasound and giving specialised care in such complex cases.



Services & Facilities

  • Specially equipped to deal with preterm deliveries with the
    state-of-the-art Neonatal SpeciallyIntensive Care Unit
  • We provide care for all pregnant patients and specialize in
    High Risk Pregnancies with the help of advanced foetal
    monitoring techniques
  • GDM (Gestational Diabetes Mellitus)
  • PIH (Pregnancy Induced Hypertension)
  • Eclampsia
  • IUGR (Intra Uterine Growth Restriction )
  • Multiple pregnancies
  • Bad obstetric history
  • Heart disease complicating pregnancy
  • Connective Tissue disorders complicating pregnancy like SLE,
    APLA
  • Haematological complications in pregnancy
  • Pain Reduced Labour Programme offered here.
    • Surgical treatment for common problems like fibroid,
      ovarian cysts etc.
    • Abdominal/Vaginal hysterectomies
    • Pelvic repair surgeries like vault repair/mesh repair
    • Reproductive endocrinology in liaison with the
      Endocrinologist
    • Family planning and contraception
    • Adolescent gynaecology
    • Menopausal/ geriatrics clinic
    • Gynaec oncology –screening for malignancy and
      management of cancer of uterus, ovary & cervix
